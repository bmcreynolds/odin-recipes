Adobo Beef

Tender Beef cooked in a spicy chipotle sauce

Ingredients

  • 3 Chipotl peppers
  • 2 tsp adobo sauce
  • 3 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp beef bouillon base
  • 1/3 cup apple cider
  • 5 roughly chopped garlic cloves
  • 3 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 1/4 tsp allpice
  • 1 tsp salt and pepper each
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 4 lb beef chuck roast
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 4 oz can of green hatch chiles
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cub beef broth
  • 2 bay leaves

Instructions

  1. Add the first 11 ingredients to a blender and process until smooth
  2. Trim large fat sections from the roast and season beef
  3. Add oil to instant pot and select 'saute'
  4. Cut the beef into 4-6 pieces and brown in the intant pot
  5. Remove the beef, and then saute the onion until soft
  6. Add 1 cup of beef broth and scrape up all the fond until incorporated
  7. Add tomato paste, green chiles, and bay leaves
  8. Turn off Saute mode and add beef
  9. Pour adobo sauce over beef
  10. Pressure cook on high for one hour
