Wolf Stew

Ingredients

  • 3lbs beef chuck roast
  • 1-2 onions
  • Lots of garlic
  • Beef broth
  • Mashed potatoes of your choice
  1. Heavily salt the beef up to 24 hours in advance. The salt absorbs into the meat, so the longer the wait the more salt you can use
  2. Julienne the onions and mince the garlic
  3. Clean off any extra salt from the waiting Beef
  4. Cut the beef into 4-6 chunks
  5. Season the beef with salt, pepper, chili powder, and whatever else. I like to use lime adobo seasoning
  6. Oil the instant pot and set to saute
  7. Saute the beef chunks until all side have a nice brown crust
  8. Add the onions to the instant pot and saute until they're soft and starting to brown
  9. Add the garlic and saute the onions and garlic until fragrant and soft
  10. Add 2 cups of beef broth the the pot, breaking up and disolving any fond
  11. Add the beef and pressure cook on high for 1hr
  12. When it's done, break apart at large chunks of meat with a fork and server with artisan bread and mashed potatoes
