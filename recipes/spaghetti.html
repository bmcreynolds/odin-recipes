Spaghetti

Delightful pasta with a tomato and beef sauce adored by kids everywhere

Ingredients

  • One box of spaghetti noodles
  • salt
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • One jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 finely diced onion
  • 3 cloves of crushed and minced garlic

Instructions

  1. Add water to a pot, using less than intructed on the pasta
  2. Heavily salt the water until it's salty but not unbearable
  3. Once the water is boiling, add the nooldes for 10 minutes
  4. Add the beef to a hot skillet. Allow to brown before smashing
  5. Once beef is brown, mash beef and mix until cooked through
  6. Move cooked beef to a new pot
  7. Add onions to the skillet and saute until soft and browning
  8. Add the garlic to the onions and saute until fragrant
  9. Move the onions and garlic to the same pot with the beef
  10. Add the jar of sauce to the meat pot and mix until fully incorporated
  11. When spaghetti is done, use tongs to move noodles from the water directly to the sauce allowing pasta water to come with it.
